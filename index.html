CleanPay is a smart waste management system using RFID cards to ensure fair payments for waste collection services. Customers make a single payment for multiple collections (e.g., 3-4 per month) and have their counts get reduced with each service. After using all their collection counts, they pay again. The system promotes recycling by sorting recyclable and non-recyclable waste. Payments and services are tracked via a website, with top-up options and confirmations through SMS or Email.
